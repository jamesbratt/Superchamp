A Digital Gran Fondo

Superchamp lets any cyclist take part in or even create their own Tour De France style cycling event right on their own door step.

Compete against your friends or other cyclists in your area, for fun and to help improve your fitness.

Explore Events

How it works

Find an event

Register then sign-in to Superchamp and either join an existing race, or create your own.

Get on your bike

Check the route for a stage you want to ride, then get out on the bike and go for it! Make sure to record your ride with a Garmin GPS device.

Upload your time

Just upload the .FIT file from your Garmin for the stage, so Superchamp can calculate your time.

Be a competitor

Every event has two jersies to compete for

Yellow Jersey

Go for glory and compete for the ultimate prize in cycling by aiming for the best overall time across the most stage finishes.

Green Jersey

Be the sprint champion and compete tactically by pushing yourself on certain route segments across all event stages.
